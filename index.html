Based in the heart of the beautiful Pokhara-32, Gagan Gaunda, Kaski, Gandaki Province, Nepal, Gogan Secondary School is a renowned educational institution. Established in 1968 AD (2025 BS), the school takes pride in being a community-driven center of learning, instilling knowledge and values in generations of students.Gogan Secondary School is affiliated with the National Examination Board (NEB) and holds recognition from the Ministry of Education. The school caters to a diverse range of educational needs, offering comprehensive programs starting from Early Childhood Development (ECD) up to Plus Two in Management, Science, and Education streams.
Affordable Quality Education in Pokhara
One of the key aspects of Gogan Secondary School is its commitment to providing affordable education. With a moderate fee structure in place, it ensures quality education is accessible to all. To further support this commitment, the school offers a scholarship scheme for deserving students, facilitating a more inclusive learning environment.
Mission and Vision
Mission
To empower students by providing high-quality, inclusive, and holistic education in a nurturing environment.
Vision
To develop students into academically successful, responsible citizens and lifelong learners who contribute positively to their communities and the world.
